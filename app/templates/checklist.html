The first 16 weeks of this program (the "residency") will happen in Washington, DC, which means that students need to live there from April 15th through August 15th.

Code for Progress will give students enough money to pay rent and bills, and to eat and live in DC during the residency in DC. We will also help you to find housing for those four months, if you don't already live in the area. Paying for other things is up to you.

After the residency, we'll have a group phone call or online community activity every week until April 1, 2014. This will take no more than half an hour each week, but it really is required (because what we do in those activities is a big part of what makes our program different).
